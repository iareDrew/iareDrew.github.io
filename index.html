About Me

As a physical security professional, I pride myself on being exceptionally organized and efficient, especially when navigating fast-paced multitasking environments. My ability to prioritize is not only effective but is executed with a unique blend of creativity, enthusiasm, and unwavering integrity. As a disciplined and conscientious self-starter, I have honed my skills in strategically planning and prioritizing tasks, ensuring that I not only meet but exceed the goals set before me. Throughout my experiences, I have consistently demonstrated resilience and poise in high-pressure situations, ensuring that every challenge is met head-on and resolved with diligence. I am eager to bring these qualities to any endeavor, ensuring success and growth every step of the way.

Resume

Starting my career as a Sergeant in the United States Marine Corps, I honed my skills in discipline, leadership, and teamwork over the span of four years. My role was pivotal in joint military operations, where I excelled in weapons handling, training, and the precise usage of military weapon systems. My tenure in the Marine Corps also exposed me to international travel, granting me a broader perspective on global security matters. Post my honorable discharge, I transitioned into the private security sector, taking on a role as a Security Guard at Alliance Security. There, my responsibilities included patrolling, ensuring public safety, handling receptionist duties, and focusing intensely on customer service and public relations. The skills I acquired over the year expanded my proficiency in the realms of physical security and customer service. My career trajectory then led me to Sottile Security Solutions Inc. as a Security Specialist. Here, my skill set blossomed with in-depth expertise in corporate security, public policy, crowd control, and mobile patrol. My rigorous discipline, coupled with my attention to public safety and physical security, ensured the optimal safeguarding of assets and personnel. The pinnacle of my journey, thus far, has been my role as a Private Military Contractor with Triple Canopy. In this position, I specialize in international travel, physical security, 24-hour emergency service, patrol security details and weapons & tactics instructions. I maintain a firm grasp on international relations and the intricacies of military weapons handling.
